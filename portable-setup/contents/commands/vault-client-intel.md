# Client Intelligence — What Should I Pitch Next?

Read the business and client sections of the Obsidian vault and generate strategic recommendations.

## Instructions

1. **Use Obsidian CLI** for fast cross-referencing:
   ```bash
   obsidian search query="client name" vault="VAULT_NAME"
   obsidian backlinks file="Client Note" vault="VAULT_NAME"
   ```

2. **Read client and business notes**:
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Business/Redbaez.md` — client roster and positioning
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Business/Uncommon Creative.md`
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Business/Alt-Shift.md`
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Mother London/Mother London.md`
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Client Work/Client Work Index.md`
   - `~/VAULT_PATH/Business/Business Documents Index.md`

3. **Read what I've already built**: Scan project notes to understand capabilities.

4. **For each active client**, analyze:
   - What I've already delivered
   - What I know about their needs
   - What capabilities I have that they're NOT using yet
   - What tools built for OTHER clients could be adapted for them

5. **Generate recommendations**:

   ### [Client Name]
   - **Current status**: What's the relationship today?
   - **Untapped opportunity**: What should I propose next?
   - **Cross-pollination**: Which tool/approach from another client applies here?
   - **Revenue potential**: Quick estimate if relevant
   - **Pitch angle**: One sentence on how to frame it

6. **Also identify**: Are there potential NEW clients that the vault suggests? Industries or agency types not yet served?

7. **Open the most actionable client note** in Obsidian:
   ```bash
   obsidian open file="Client Engagement" vault="VAULT_NAME"
   ```
