## How the skill uses this directory

1. **Pre-pitch verification:** For each named contact, run `WebSearch` to confirm they are still in role at the listed publication. Editorial roles in AU trade press churn yearly. Update this file when a discrepancy is found.

2. **Tier-based send sequencing:** Tier 1 outlets get the exclusive first (24-48hr lead time). Tier 2 outlets receive the broadcast with a hook: "Here's what [Tier 1 outlet] just covered, here's why it's actually bigger."

3. **Outlet-specific pitch shape:**
   - Mumbrella: opinion piece submission to Nathan Jolly, OR news pitch to Vivienne Kelly. Choose based on whether the story is "Tom's argument" or "Tom's news."
   - Unmade: industry-intel framing, not product pitch. Land the architecture as analysis.
   - AdNews: news framing, media-buyer angle if possible.
   - B&T: creative-circuit framing, awards-relevant if applicable.
   - Campaign Brief: creative work showcase, not architecture explainer.

4. **Never broadcast same content to all five.** Each pitch is rewritten for the specific outlet's editorial voice. Same canonical description in the body, different opener and CTA.
