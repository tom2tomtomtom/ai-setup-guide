## Voice rules applied

- No em-dashes throughout (used commas, periods, or rewrites)
- No agency-speak (no "leverage", "unlock", "ladder up", "best-in-class", "north star")
- Direct claims, specific numbers, anecdotal frame
- Closing CTA is the URL, not a question
- First-person voice, no third-person product description
- Read each version aloud before posting. If a phrase makes you wince, kill it.
